Module 5: High Volume Grading

While having modern material is important, every language has classics that cannot be ignored. You cannot learn Ancient Greek without reading Homer, learn Latin without Virgil. Even modern languages have important classics like Don Quixote and The Count of Monte Cristo. This module will show how to use LLMs to capture the essence of these classics and teach them to your students.

LLMs can assist in language learning in the following ways:


- Grammar Analysis: LLMs can identify and correct grammatical errors in written text.


- Vocabulary Enhancement: They suggest synonyms and provide explanations for word choices, enriching language use.


- Coherence Assessment: LLMs evaluate the overall flow and coherence of written pieces, offering insights into narrative structure.

Writing essays

Assignment:

"Write a short paragraph (150-200 words) describing your favorite childhood memory. Reflect on the sights, sounds, and emotions that made this memory special to you."

Instructions

"After completing your paragraph, submit it for automated analysis by the Language Model. The LLM will provide feedback on grammar correctness, vocabulary usage, and overall coherence. Pay attention to the suggestions and insights provided by the LLM to enhance your writing skills."

Dialogue

Assignment:

"Engage in a dialogue with a virtual conversational partner on a given topic. The goal is to practice conversational language, and the dialogue will be analyzed by the Language Model for language proficiency."

Instructions

"Access the virtual conversational platform provided, where you'll find prompts for dialogues. Select a topic and initiate a dialogue with the virtual partner. The conversation should flow naturally. The Language Model will assess grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, and overall conversational coherence."

Listening Comprehension

Assignment:

"Listen to a selected podcast episode on a specific topic and analyze it for language comprehension. Utilize the Language Model to assess your understanding and identify areas for improvement."

Instructions

" Access the designated podcast episode provided for this activity. After listening, summarize the main points, key ideas, and any supporting details from the podcast. Submit your summary for analysis by the Language Model. "
