Module 6: Manual Review

Despite the power of LLMs shown through the course, it is still important to manually review your students work. This module will go over how to monitor LLM output, check for inaccuracy and catch what the model may have missed.

Idiomatic Expressions

Example: Idiomatic expressions unique to a language or region may not be accurately interpreted by LLMs. Manual review ensures that idioms are appropriately understood and evaluated.

Figurative Language:

Example: Figurative language, such as metaphors or similes, can be challenging for LLMs to interpret accurately. Manual review is essential for recognizing and appreciating the figurative elements in a student's writing.

Situational Context:

Example: A response that depends heavily on the situational context might be misinterpreted by an LLM without a broader understanding of the scenario. Manual review ensures a nuanced evaluation.
